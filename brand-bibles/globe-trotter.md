# Globe-Trotter — Brand Bible

> Heritage British luggage maker, est. 1897. Vulcanised Fibreboard cases hand-built in England. Carry-ons retail £1,795–£2,595. Audience: legacy luxury / cultural-elite (queens, prime ministers, James Bond licensees, F1 partners).

## Voice & Tone

- **Formality:** refined-editorial. Long-form copy reads like a memoir or museum wall text, not e-commerce.
- **Sentiment:** understated, authoritative, romantic-without-sentimental. "A Companion to Discovery." "A Meeting of Italian Precision and British Legacy."
- **Recurring vocabulary (verified, lifted from site):**
  - heritage, character, characterful, companion, journey, voyage, adventure
  - hand / handmade / hand-built / by hand / craftsmanship
  - timeless, enduring, unchanged, since 1897 / 125 years / 129 years
  - Vulcanised Fibreboard, England, Victorian, Golden Age (of travel)
  - bespoke / custom, limited, exclusive, archive
  - "Join the Journey" (newsletter CTA — avoids "subscribe" / "sign up for offers")
- **Person/voice:** plural first-person institutional ("we", "our", "ours"). Brand speaks as a house, not a startup. Names dropped factually, never gushingly: Queen Elizabeth II, Winston Churchill, Edmund Hillary, Daniel Craig, David Beckham, Kate Moss, Emma Watson [src: /pages/about-us].
- **Never says (inferred from absence across all scraped pages):**
  - No discount language: no "sale", "% off", "deal", "limited-time offer", "free gift with purchase", "BOGO", "while stocks last". Only "Regular price £X" appears — ever.
  - No urgency / FOMO triggers: no "selling fast", "only X left", countdowns.
  - No tech-spec marketing-speak: no "innovation™", "next-gen", "smart luggage", "AI-powered".
  - No lifestyle hyperbole: no "game-changer", "must-have", "obsessed", "iconic" (used factually only re: design).
  - No emoji, no exclamation marks, no all-caps shouting in body copy.
  - No "founder story" Instagram-DTC voice. No "we" as in "we built this in our garage."
  - No sustainability sermon — sustainability implied through "5-year warranty / repair service / cases that outlive owners," never preached.

**Confidence: verified** (homepage, heritage page, FPM landing, journal index — all consistent).

## Visual Mood

Two distinct visual registers, deployed in parallel:

**Register A — Lifestyle / campaign (colour, location, single figure):**
- Palette: warm Parisian-stone neutrals (cream, ecru, oatmeal wool), oxblood / chocolate brown / cognac (the case), charcoal black, soft grey cobble. Cool dawn-grey sky.
- Lighting: overcast diffused daylight, north-European, no harsh sun. No golden hour. No fill flash. Low contrast, high mid-tone richness.
- Composition: wide cinematic 16:9, single figure mid-stride, three-quarter rear or profile, generous negative space (architecture). Subject not centred; off-axis. [src: home_hero `Homepage-FPM-Desktop.jpg`]
- Texture codes: woven wool, smooth leather strapping, riveted metal corners, cobblestone, limestone façade.

**Register B — Studio product (moody, monochrome ground, no human):**
- Palette: deep ocean green, navy, oxblood, ivory — all set against single-tone seamless backgrounds (forest green, near-black, ivory).
- Lighting: large soft-box top-side, controlled fall-off, hint of rim. Close to chiaroscuro. No catchlights chasing. [src: fpm_hero studio shot]
- Composition: dead-centre, symmetrical, museum-object framing. Product is the icon, not a prop.

**Register C — Heritage editorial (B&W archival):**
- Used on /pages/about-us. High-grain monochrome of tailored male figures with cases on staircases / boarding scenes. Reads as commissioned 1960s-style fashion editorial mixed with family-archive photos. [src: heritage page screenshot]

- **Photography vs. illustration vs. CGI mix:** ~95% photography (lifestyle + studio), ~5% archival B&W. No illustration. No CGI/3D. No motion graphics on owned channels visible.

**Confidence: verified** for registers A & B (downloaded hero assets); register C **inferred from screenshot only** (hero images not downloaded individually).

## ❌ NO-GO LIST (absolute rules)

Inferred from conspicuous absence across every scraped surface. For this brand, restraint IS the editorial. Violating any of these would read as a counterfeit Globe-Trotter ad.

**Linguistic / copy:**
- ❌ No discount language ("sale", "%", "off", "deal", "free", "BOGO", "limited-time")
- ❌ No urgency / FOMO ("only X left", "selling fast", countdown timers)
- ❌ No exclamation marks, no emoji, no all-caps body copy, no rhetorical questions
- ❌ No founder-DTC tone ("Hey traveler!", "We made this for you")
- ❌ No tech-marketing buzzwords ("smart", "AI", "next-gen", "revolutionary")
- ❌ No sustainability preaching (it's implied via repair / 5-yr warranty / longevity, never sermonised)
- ❌ No price-led messaging at all — ads must lead with story or product, never price

**Visual / atmospheric:**
- ❌ No neon, no fluorescent colour, no high-saturation grading
- ❌ No cold-blue tech grading; no orange-and-teal blockbuster look
- ❌ No extreme weather: no snow storms, no breath-vapour clouds, no rain-soaked streets, no desert heat-haze, no jungle, no beach. Globe-Trotter is **not survival gear**.
- ❌ No street smoke, steam vents, dystopian / cyberpunk aesthetics
- ❌ No motion blur from the camera (subject blur from movement is acceptable; shaky-cam is not)
- ❌ No crowd scenes, no airport queues, no real travel chaos
- ❌ No party / nightlife / bar settings
- ❌ No food shots, no hotel breakfast tables (wrong category cues)
- ❌ No pets / animals (other than charity collab e.g. Elephant Family — and that's editorial, not lifestyle hero)

**Character / casting:**
- ❌ No children, no teenagers
- ❌ No smiling-to-camera. No laughing. No "candid" giggles. Composure only.
- ❌ No group-hug / friend-pile / squad shots
- ❌ No fitness / athletic / sportswear casting
- ❌ No streetwear, hoodies, graphic tees, visible third-party logos
- ❌ No PDA / romantic kissing scenes
- ❌ No phone-in-hand / scrolling shots (anachronistic to brand world)

**Format / channel:**
- ❌ No meme formats, no trend-jacking, no remix-audio TikTok templates
- ❌ No influencer "unboxing" energy. (Reveals are slow, ceremonial, archival — not unboxing.)
- ❌ No comparison ads ("vs. RIMOWA", "better than…")
- ❌ No before/after, no testimonials with star ratings, no "as seen in" badge clutter

**Confidence: inferred-but-strong.** Every item on this list is supported by *consistent absence* across homepage, heritage, FPM, centenary, and journal index. Any single brand surface containing one of these would lower confidence; none did.

## Ad-format ecosystem & strategic gap

**Currently running (verified):**
- Meta: at least one active static brand-owned ad in JP market (Library ID 3072495283139533, started 9 Apr 2026, JP language: "129年間変わることのない、冒険の精神。世代を超えて受け継がれる、一生以上の価値。"). Format: still creative + Learn More.
- Web: hero banner image (16:9 lifestyle), product grid stills, journal long-form text/image articles.
- Owned IG / TikTok / YT: existence acknowledged via footer links; content not scrapable in this audit.

**Conspicuous gap (the pitch wedge):**

1. **9:16 cinematic vertical short-form** is missing or invisible. Brand has the visual language (locked-off frames, single poised figure, European architecture) ready-made for Reels/TikTok/Shorts but likely is not running paid 9:16 video in volume. This is the standout opportunity for an AI-generated UGC-but-luxury-grade pipeline.

2. **Heritage / craftsmanship long-form video** — the journal copy already references "Why craftsmanship matters in the digital age," "Meet The Craftsmen," "Through The Lens" — but corresponding 30s / 60s film cuts aren't surfaced. There is appetite for it; supply seems thin.

3. **Multi-market localisation in motion** — JP ad is text-only static. Globe-Trotter's most loyal modern market (Japan) is given the least dynamic ad creative.

4. **Performance creative discipline** — given the no-go list, performance-style creative (price-led, urgency-led, discount-led) is structurally forbidden for this brand. The strategic gap is therefore *brand-led performance creative*: short-form video that performs against Meta/IG/TikTok algorithms while obeying every line of the no-go list. That's a hard creative brief that almost no agency can fulfil at scale — which is the wedge.

**Recommended pitch format:** 9:16 / 30s / locked-off / single figure in European stone-architecture setting / oxblood case / no dialogue / cello or solo-piano score / one or two cuts max / final beat is the case alone, centred, on coloured seamless. Built modular so the same beats re-cut to 6s / 15s / 30s.

**Confidence: inferred** for the gap — conditional on IG/TikTok/YT scrape returning no contradictory volume.
